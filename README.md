# Arisa Bot - Privacy Policy
Last Updated: April 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community by using Arisa ("Bot", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us via our official Support Server.

When you invite our Bot to your Discord server and use its services, you trust us with your server data and limited personal information necessary for the bot to function. This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our Bot.

1. Information We Collect
We collect the minimum amount of data required to provide our main features: Server Management, Statistics Tracking, and Music Streaming.

We automatically collect and temporarily store the following data:

Discord IDs: Your User ID, Guild (Server) ID, and Channel IDs.
Presence Data: Your current online status (Online/Idle/DND) and Rich Presence/Activity data (e.g., games you are playing or music you are listening to). This is only used dynamically and is not permanently stored.
Voice & Message Statistics: We track the duration you spend in voice channels and the number of messages you send to generate analytical server leaderboards. We do NOT store or log the content of your messages.
Invite Metrics: Data pertaining to who invited whom to the server, used strictly for the invite-tracking leaderboard module.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data exclusively to power the Bot's core components:

Providing real-time /userinfo scanners and dynamic user profiles.
Operating the 
StatManager
 to calculate and display your weekly/monthly server activity ranks.
Powering the Auto-Role module by assigning roles based on your Rich Presence activity or server join status.
Debugging errors and ensuring platform stability without distributing this data elsewhere.
3. Data Retention & Deletion
The data (IDs and generated analytics/numbers) is stored securely within our private database.

You have the right to request the deletion of your data (or your server's data) at any time.
If the Bot is kicked or removed from a server, all primary configuration settings and specific server states are eventually wiped from our active caches.
If you request data wiping, we will completely purge all your statistical arrays within our database.
4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal information or Discord user data to third parties. The bot relies on secure official third-party integrations automatically required for operations:

Discord API: To function legitimately on your server.
YouTube / Spotify Extractors: To securely stream music without logging your personal playback habits.
MongoDB: To encrypt and securely save your server statistics.
5. Contact Us
If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to request data deletion, you may reach out to our Developer Team by joining the official Arisa Support Server on Discord.

By continuing to use Arisa in your server, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy.
